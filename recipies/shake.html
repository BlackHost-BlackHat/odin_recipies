Protein is in its heyday right now, meaning it’s more important than ever to learn how to make a protein shake…that’s actually good.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup protein powder vanilla flavored
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup ice

Steps:

  1. In a high speed blender, add all the protein powder, milk, and ice. Add your flavor mix-ins and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass or protein shaker and enjoy immediately.
  3. Storage: Protein shakes are best enjoyed fresh, but needed, they can be stored in a sealed container in the fridge for up to one day. Give it a good stir before serving.
